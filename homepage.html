Class Homepage

Description

The goal for this piece was to design the look of my class homepage for Interactive Design I. I decided to embrace an 8-bit styled Mario theme in pretty much every way possible. Everything from the navigation bar, to the border images, and even in the background are all filled to the brim with references and fun details

Details

Context: Interactive Design I Project

Software: Brackets, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator

Time Spent: 2-3 Weeks

Typefaces: Rock'n'roll One, Instrument Sans

Link: http://vbitbws.tcc.edu/~arty6/
