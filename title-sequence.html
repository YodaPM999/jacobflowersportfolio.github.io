Title Sequence

Description

The goal for this piece was to recreate the opening title sequence for “Dr. Seuss’ How The Grinch Stole Christmas! (1966). I aimed for an unsettling, mysterious vibe that makes the audience wonder ”Who is this Grinch and what is his deal?”

Details

Context: Motion Graphics Project

Software: Adobe After Effects

Time Spent: 4-5 Weeks

Video Duration: 1 minute, 18 seconds

Quality: Full HD (1920 x 1080)

Background Audio: Mafioso (Purple Planet Music)
