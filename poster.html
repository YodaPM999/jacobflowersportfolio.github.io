Theater Poster

Description

The goal for this piece was to design a poster for a theoretical stage play based on the fairy tale “Hans In Luck” using a rhetorical device to convey the meaning of the story. In my design, I show the main character, Hans, struggling to carry all of his metaphorical “burdens” up a steep slope.

Details

Context: Computer Graphics II Project

Software: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

Time Spent: 3-4 Weeks

Typefaces: Mr Eaves Mod OT, Mr Eaves San OT

Size: 16 in x 20 in
