Space Journey

Description

The goal for this piece was to create an educational website about the planets in our solar system. Since my target audience was aimed for kids around the ages 8-10, I embraced the silly, giving each planet their own facial expression and personality. I also decided to give everything a glowing effect to further capture the feeling of being in outer space.

Details

Context: Interactive Design II Project

Software: Brackets, Adobe illustrator

Time Spent: 2-3 Weeks

Typefaces: Rowdies Regular, Arimo Regular

Link: http://vbitbws.tcc.edu/~arta6/projects/project_3/index.html
