Identity System

Description

The goal of this project was to create an entire identity system for the fictional small business Dolightful Donuts. Everything from the logo to the business cards had to be designed in a way that reflects the company brand.

Details

Context: Computer Graphics II Project

Software: Adobe Illustrator

Time Spent: 2 Months

Typefaces: Gill Sans Nova, Gill Sans MT

Sizes: Logo - 4 in x 6 in, Business Card - 2 in x 3.5 in, Letterhead - 8.5 in x 11 in, Envelope - 9.5 in x 4.125 in
