Five

Validate, validate, validate.
Validation is the process of verifying that a document adheres to the rules of the language used in the document. Valid pages are more accessible, easier to maintain, and ensures that your pages are compatible with current and future browsers. Validate often:

  • HTML: HTML Validator
  • CSS: CSS Validator
  • W3C Link Checker: Link Checker
